Kasim Kabbara

Kasim Kabbara is a Senior majoring in African American Diaspora Studies and minoring in Radio-Television-Film. He has talents in Photography, Videography and Acting. During his time at UT, he has been named one of "UT's Upcoming Entrepreneurs" for his photography business "IceVisuals," has worked as a photographer for the school's newspaper, and has served as President of the Black Student Alliance.He plans on pursuing a career in Sports Broadcasting post graduation.
