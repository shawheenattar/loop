Who We Are


At Loop, we believe that no matter the idea, anyone can express their vision in a meaningful way. That's why we want to give college students the chance to create original, creative, and meaningful content for the real world.


Our goal is to change the way college students work. Experience and action are the best ways to prove skill in the modern world, and we believe giving students those opportunities will provide our clients with the best possible experience and work.
